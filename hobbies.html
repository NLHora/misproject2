Hobbies Page

Skiing

I’ve been skiing my entire life, and in high school I made the decision to join my school’s alpine ski team, one of the best decisions I have ever made. Over my three years on the team, I not only refined my skiing, but I also developed skills in leadership, confidence, and collaboration. During my senior year, I had the honor of becoming a captain, which involved motivating others, leading drills and stretching, organizing events, and many other duties. Additionally, balancing school and ski taught me more about time management with my academics. I am looking forward to skiing for the Duluth alpine ski team while continuing to grow as a person and skier.

Hiking and Camping

Hiking has been an important part of my life, as my family often takes trips around the country to explore national parks and experience beautiful nature. This past summer, however, I had the opportunity to take a camping trip to Yellowstone and the Grand Tetons with a few friends. The trip was extremely challenging and memorable, it taught me lots about planning, independence, and problem-solving. The most memorable part of the experience was hiking to Delta Lake, a strenuous eight-mile round trip hike with over 2,000 feet of elevation gain. It was the most difficult hike I have ever completed, yet the most rewarding. Reaching the top after many hours was a feeling I will never forget and hope to experience again as I hike more in the future.
